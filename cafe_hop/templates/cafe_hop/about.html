Cafe Hop was created by Cal Alumn, Rahul Doraiswami, and 4th year student, Ji-hern Baek. It exists as a crowdsourcing platform for Cal students and general Berkeley residents with respect to cafe fullness. Viewers can input their observations in the form of a percentage. Cafe Hop will, then, provide a weighted moving average of ratings from the last 60 minutes.




Let us know if you have any questions, comments, or suggestions at: cafehopberkeley@gmail.com.
